Contemporary

Contemporary is a modern dance form developed in the mid-twentieth century. It has grown to become a dominant genre in the world. It focuses on the western techniques of contemporary dancing. Our course is customised to ages of 18 onwards.

We have two types of courses under Contemporary:

Level 1 – Beginners
Starting out and want to gain the Basics & Core Fundamentals of the art form? This course is perfect for you.

Level 2 – Advanced
You have the moves and you want to take yourself to the next level. Let us help you bring out your A-Game.



“The rhythm is in your blood, can you feel it?”

Hip Hop

Hip Hop refers to street dance styles primarily performed to hip-hop music or that have evolved as part of the hip-hop culture. Our course is designed for ages of 18 onwards. It focuses on building your grooves, bounce, footwork and style of Hip Hop dancing taught through our choreography model. This takes you through a step by step routine giving you the maximum development.

We have two types of courses under Hip Hop:

Level 1 – Beginners
Do you have the passion and energy which is most suited for Hip Hop? Let us help you channel that energy.

Level 2 – Advanced
Your footwork has improved but you think you can kick it up a notch? Hop on.



“Dance is the conversation between body and soul”

Belly Dance

Belly Dance is an expressive dance which emphasizes complex movements of the torso. Originally a Middle Eastern folk dance, it has evolved to take many different forms depending on the country and region, both in costume and dance style. Our course caters to the age group of 18 onwards. We have a goal oriented course which helps you build on the technique and develop your skills rapidly.

Bollywood

Bollywood is the dance-form used in the Indian films. It is a mixture of numerous styles. These styles include belly-dancing, Kathak, Indian folk, Western popular etc. We will help you get in tune with the famous Indian numbers and the moves for them. Our course is designed for ages of 18 onwards.

Yoga

Yoga is the ancient Indian practice which revolves around Fitness, Flexibility and Healthy Living. We will help you find your physical, meditative and spiritual core. Our course caters to ages of 18 onwards.

Zumba

Zumba involves dance and aerobic movements performed to energetic music. The choreography incorporates Hip Hop, Soca, Samba, Salsa, Merengue and Mambo. Squats and lunges are also included. This course gets you fit and in shape with the most effective way while developing your dancing skills. Our course is designed for ages of 18 onwards.
