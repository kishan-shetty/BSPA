About Us

Performing arts has often proven to be a medium for self-expression, confidence, compassion and higher academic achievement. Bangalore School of Performing Arts’ vision is to bring an array of performing arts for progressive learning under one roof to help nurture your creative side. We have a young and bright ambience that ensures art, performance and daily activities are one’s best friend.
