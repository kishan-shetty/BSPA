Courses - Kids

Dance Active

Dance Active is our curriculum specially tailored for kids below the ages of 3 to 7. This customised course helps your child gain skills in dancing, these include elements of active play, gymnastics and theatre, which enhances their ability much faster. It ensures your child develops these skills with fun and gains an exponential learning curve!

Contemporary

Contemporary is a modern dance form developed in the mid-twentieth century. It has grown to become a dominant genre in the world. Our course caters to ages of 7 onwards. It focuses on the western techniques of contemporary dancing for beginner level dancers. The course aims to develop your child by giving the right core fundamentals and inculcating the basic disciplines of the popular art form.

Hip Hop

Hip Hop refers to street dance styles primarily performed to hip-hop music or that have evolved as part of the hip-hop culture. Focus is given on developing the grooves, bounce, footwork and Hip Hop dancing which is taught through our choreography model for beginners. A choreography model gives focus on learning step by step to achieve a larger routine for your child. Our course caters to ages of 7 onwards.

Kathak

Kathak is one of the ten major forms of Indian dance. This classical form of dancing enables story-telling through dance. The origin of Kathak is traditionally attributed to the traveling bards of ancient northern India, known as Kathakars or storytellers. Kathak dance emphasizes rhythmic foot movements, adorned with small bells also known as ‘Ghungroo’, with the movement being harmonized to the music. We’ll help your child express himself through this art form. Our course caters to ages of 4 onwards.

Ballet

Ballet is a western classical dance form which is a highly technical with its own vocabulary based on French terminology. It has been globally influential and has defined the foundational techniques used in many other dance genres. Starting your child young helps in developing the right movement, footwork and balance which are the fundamentals of Ballet. Our course is designed for age groups of 4 onwards.

Gymnastics

Gymnastics is a fitness art form that focuses on flexibility, strength and body conditioning through a set of gymnastic based principles. Although it focuses on building the physical core of any individual; alertness, precision, daringness, self-confidence and self-discipline are mental traits that are also developed for your child through gymnastics. Our course is tailored for ages of 4 onwards.

Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art form which is characterized by its emphasis on head-height kicks, jumping & spinning kicks, and fast kicking techniques. It is recognised by the Indian Taekwondo Association and trains kids in self-defence. Our course is for age groups of 4 onwards.

Theatre

Theatre is the art of emoting, acting and performance. Modern theatre, broadly defined, includes performances of plays and musical theatre. There are connections between theatre and the art forms of ballet, opera (which uses staged, costumed performances with singing and orchestral accompaniment) and various other forms. It is a structured course to introduce kids to theatre/acting and develop their self-awareness to be more comfortable on stage. This encourages them in developing the skill of expression. Our course is tailored to ages of 4 onwards.
